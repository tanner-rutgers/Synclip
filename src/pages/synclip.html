User Guide


Synclip allows you to sync your clipboard text between any computers running Chrome. Simply copy text to your clipboard, click "Sync" on the extension popup, and immediately get notified on all other computers where you're signed in and have the extension installed. The text can then be easily copied from the notification. Synclip also stores a history of previously shared clipboards!

Synclip is designed to be very simple and ready to use, but just in case, here's a step-by-step guide.



Prerequisites

  1. Make sure you're signed in to Chrome
    • In order for Synclip to work, you must be signed in to Chrome on the device you want to share your clipboard from, and any device you want to share your clipboard to. For details on signing in to Chrome click here.
  2. Sync your extensions
    • Synclip uses extension sync storage to sync your clipboard. This means you must have extension synchronization enabled in Chrome.
      1. In the top-right corner of the screen, click the Chrome menu
      2. Click Settings.
      3. Under "Sign In," click Advanced sync settings.
        • To sync all data, select Sync everything from the dropdown.
        • To sync only your extensions (required), select Choose what to sync from the dropdown, then check the checkbox next to Extensions.
      4. Click OK.

Syncing clipboard

  1. Click the Synclip extension icon
  2. Your current clipboard content is shown in the clipboard window. To sync, simply click the Sync button.
  3. All other computers signed into the same user profile on Chrome should immediately show a notification with the synced content ready to copy.
    • Simply click Copy on the notification to copy the content to the clipboard on that computer.
    • You can also choose to dismiss the notification by clicking the Dismiss button (don't worry, the content will be available in your history for you to copy later).

Viewing history

Synclip stores a clearable history of your last 5 synchronized clipboards in the History tab.

  1. Click the Synclip extension icon
  2. Click History at the top right of the popup.
  3. Your most recent clipboard is shown in the clipboard window. You can navigate between previous clipboards using the arrow buttons at the bottom of the popup.
  4. To copy content from the history into your clipboard, simply click Copy when the content you want to copy is in the history clipboard window.
  5. To clear your history, click Clear history at the bottom of the popup.

General sync issues

If you're not receiving notifications on other PCs after syncing, your clipboard history isn't viewable, or the popup appears disabled, please visit the User Guide to ensure you've completed the necessary prerequisites and are using the extension properly.


Error message when syncing

If you see the error message Error syncing clipboard, you have likely exceeded one of the following limitations of chrome sync storage, the mechanism that Synclip uses to sync clipboards:

  • Each clipboard has a maximum storage size of 8162 characters. Try syncing a smaller clipboard if you suspect this is the issue.
  • Chrome sync storage is limited to 1800 operations per hour or 120 operations per minute. Synclip may use multiple operations per sync. Try waiting a few seconds or minutes if you suspect this is the issue.
